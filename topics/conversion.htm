Well conversion

 

 

 

 

Well conversion can be handled by clicking on the "New wells / conversion" icon in Precept Main interface.

 

Hard date can be specified for the conversion, or the conversion can be linked to an event.

 

 

In the example above, D-WAG1 is a WAG well. The well is converted to Gas Injector on 01/07/2023 and then every 12 months.

The well is converted to Water Injector on 01/01/2024 and then every 12 months.

D-WAG1 is therefore a gas injector for 6 months, then an water Injector for 6 months, then Gas injector for 6 months, etc..

 

 

In the example above, the well K-W2, will be converted to Water Injector 30 days after (note the minus sign) the Opportunity 'K-W3' is executed. In that case, the conversion date is not "hard', but linked to the sequence of events, allowing fast generation of what-if scenario when sequence of events is modified.
