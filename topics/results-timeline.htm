Forecast results: Timeline

 

 

 

 

Timeline is a very powerful way to QC and analyse the results of the forecast.

Simply move hover the mouse to get more details about the status of the nodes and wells at any timestep.

 

QC and analyzes of a forecast should include:

 - understanding of bottlenecks in production network

 - make sure shutdown planning is accurate.

 - make sure new profiles actually comes online at the right time

 - etc..
