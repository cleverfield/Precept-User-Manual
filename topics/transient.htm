Transient

 

 

 

 

The option to take into account transient production can be activated by clicking on the "Settings" icon in Precept Main User Interface and check the "Capture transient production" option.

 

 

When this option is activated, Precept will adjust the production and injection potentials of wells after a shutdown occurs, allowing:

 - temporary increase of production potential from producer wells (due to transient production)-

 - temporary decrease of injection potential for injectors wells (due to bean-up) to reach again full injection potentials

 

 

Required inputs need to be entered by clicking the "Transient" icon in the DCA module (see Decline curves: Production and Injection potentials)
