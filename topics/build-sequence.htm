Build sequence of events

 

 

 

 

Once created, Opportunities are by default placed in the "Pool" of opportunity. BY default the pool of opportunity is not visible.

To make it visible, check the 'Show Pool of opportunity" option on the top right of Precept Main User Interface.

 

 

Move a single Opportunity called "Opportunity A" from one sequence to a new location before an opportunity called "Opportunity B"

1. Left Click on the "Opportunity A". "Opportunity A" will then be highlighted in blue.

2. Now Right-click. (Right-Click acts like "cut", "Opportunity A" will disappear)

3. Left-click on "Opportunity B" to highlight where you want to place "Opportunity A".  "Opportunity B" will be highlighted in blue.

4. Now Double-click (Double-click acts like "Paste"). "Opportunity A" will now be located before "Opportunity B".

 

 

Move several Opportunities from one sequence to a new location before an opportunity called "Opportunity B"

1. Select all the opportunities you want to move. The opportunities will then be highlighted in blue.

2. Now Right-click. (Right-Click acts like "cut", the selected opportunities will disappear)

3. Left-click on "Opportunity B" to highlight where you want to place the Opportunities.  "Opportunity B" will be highlighted in blue.

4. Now Double-click (Double-click acts like "Paste"). The opportunities will now be located before "Opportunity B".
