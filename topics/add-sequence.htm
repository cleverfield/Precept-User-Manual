Add / Delete sequence of events

 

 

 

 

Sequence of events can be added/deleted by clicking on the "Sequence", then "Add / modify" icon from Precept Main User Interface.

 

 

A sequence of events can be:

 - drilling of new wells

 - facilities maintenances performed by a maintenance crew

 - Well head maintenances performed by a Well Services crew

 - Stimulation of several wells performed by Stimulation crew

 - etc..

 

In the example below, 5 sequence of events have been introduced

- 3 rigs are going to drill new wells

- A maintenance team will be mobilized on 01/05/2023

- A construction team will be mobilized on 01/04/2023

 

 

Opportunity can then be added to the sequence of events (see Build sequence of events) to generate the schedule below.

 

 

"Permute" option allows User to modify the layout of the sequence of events (for example, Rig first, then Maintenance, then Construction) for visualization purposes.
