PVT

 

 

 

 

PVT can be entered by clicking on PVT icon in the Decline Curves Analysis (see Decline curves: Production and Injection potentials) module.

 

PVT are used in Precept to calculate production and injection at reservoir conditions for voidage calculations.

API data are used for API tracking

 

.

 

For a producer: Production Rate (rb) = Oil (std) * Bo  +  Water (std) * bw  + Free gas (std) * Bg  ,  with Free gas = Produced Gas - Rs * Oil

 

For injector: Injection Rate (rb) = Water Injection (std) * Bwi + Gas Injection (std) * Bgi

 

 

Production Rate and Injection Rate at reservoir conditions are then used to calculate voidage.

Voidage calculations require patterns to be defined. (see Patterns)
