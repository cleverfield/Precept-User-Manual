New profiles

 

 

 

 

New profiles coming online are usually linked to an Opportunity placed in the sequence of events.

 

 

However, introducing a new profile with a "hard' start date can be done by clicking on the "New profiles" icon from Precept Main User Interface.

 

 

 

 

In the example above, A1-I1 and A1-I2 are two future wells. Their start date is not "hard'. They will come online once the Opportunity A1-I1 and A1-I2 have been executed respectively. They have been introduced by creating Opportunities (see Opportunities)

 

A1-W1 is s new profile. Its Start Date is "Hard" and set to 01/04/2023.
